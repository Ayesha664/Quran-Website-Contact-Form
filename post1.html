How to Start Madani Qaida at Home for Kids

Teaching Madani Qaida at home can be easy if you follow simple steps. Here are some tips from my experience as a Quran teacher:

1. Start with Arabic Alphabets

Introduce your child to Arabic letters one by one. Use flashcards or printable sheets to make learning fun.

2. Focus on Correct Pronunciation

Always pronounce the letters correctly and have your child repeat after you. This builds a strong foundation for Tajweed later.
